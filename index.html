#IndyTechWeek

October 11th-19th, 2012

Indy Tech Week is an accidental (but awesome) week-long techstravanganza in Indianapolis. 5 events, 8 days, neato.

The Events

  • re:build

    October 11th-12th

    re:build is a conference about building the web. It's for people who make the web, and love doing it. It doesn't matter if you're a designer, a developer, or something else: as long as you're obsessed with how we build things for the web, then you should be there.

    rebuildconf.com @rebuildconf

  • Broad Ripple Music Fest

    October 13th

    The 6th annual Broad Ripple Music Fest will invade Indy's favorite cultural community mecca on Saturday, October 13th. The historic Broad Ripple Village will be the canvas for over 100 local, regional and national artists performing on 12+ stages.

    broadripplemusicfest.com @broadripplefest

  • Indy Hack Day

    October 14th

    Indiana has a talented community of developers and designers. But we don't have an event we can all attend. We thought it would be great to get together to work on our projects, see and learn from what others are doing.

    indyhackday.com @indyhackday

  • Connections

    October 16th-18th

    Connections is 3 jam-packed inspirational, educational days, from October 16-18. We’ll have over 40 breakout sessions and 8 different tracks, with more B2B and B2C-specific content than ever before. On Tuesday, you’ll hear from our keynote speaker, the Emmy and Golden Globe award-winning actor Michael J. Fox. On Wednesday evening, you’ll enjoy a private concert with Grammy-nominated band, The Fray.

    www.connections2012.com @exacttarget

  • Powder Keg

    October 17th-19th

    This conference is for the crazy ones. The ones who sometimes ignore the rules. The ones who strike forth to boldly pursue their passion, and in doing so, ignite the genius in others. We're packing the limited space in the Powder Keg with an explosive combination of entrepreneurial energy and creative talent to grow together.

    powderkeg.org @powderkegconf
