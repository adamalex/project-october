Indy Tech Week

Compiled list of tech-related (and a couple non-tech related) events happening in Indianapolis from October 11th through October 19th (2012)

EVENTS

Connections 2012

Connections is three jam-packed inspirational, educational days, from October 16-18 in Indianapolis, IN. We’ll have over 40 breakout sessions and 8 different tracks, with more B2B and B2C-specific content than ever before. On Tuesday, you’ll hear from our keynote speaker, the Emmy and Golden Globe award-winning actor Michael J. Fox. On Wednesday evening, you’ll also enjoy a private concert with Grammy-nominated band, The Fray.

View details

Powder Keg

This conference is for the crazy ones. The ones who sometimes ignore the rules. The ones who strike forth to boldly pursue their passion, and in doing so, ignite the genius in others. We're packing the limited space in the Powder Keg with an explosive combination of entrepreneurial energy and creative talent to grow together.

View details

re:build

re:build is a conference about building the web. It's for people who make the web, and love doing it. It doesn't matter if you're a designer, a developer, or something else: as long as you're obsessed with how we build things for the web, then you should be there.

View details

Indy Hack Day

Indiana has a talented community of developers and designers. But we don't have an event we can all attend. We thought it would be great to get together to work on our projects, see and learn from what others are doing.

View details

Broad Ripple Music Fest

Broad Ripple Music Fest (BRMF) is a celebration of local music, right in the heart of Indianapolis! Now in our 6th year, we’re looking at an even brighter future than ever.

View details

VENUES

Harrison Center for the arts!

The Harrison Center for the Arts is a powerful force for cultural development in Indianapolis and a model for community arts, education, and urban revitalization programs across America. We address community problems with cultural solutions: art, music, education, and grassroots activism. We cultivate emerging art patrons by redefining the boundaries of artist and audience: everyone is part of the art.

View details

The Speak Easy

The Speak Easy is a place for Indianapolis-based entrepreneurs, startups, and the folks who support them to work, play, and collaborate.

It's essentially a clubhouse for entrepreneurs, designers, and geeks. We believe that bringing these folks together in a fun, productive work environment is the surest way to accelerate the development and launch of high-growth startups in Central Indiana.

View details

JW Marriott

The JW Marriott, a premier Indianapolis convention center hotel, is part of the new $450 million Marriott Place, consisting of five Marriott hotels all connected to the Indiana Convention Center. Boasting 33 floors, our hotel is the tallest in the state and our 1,005 guest rooms make us the largest JW Marriott in the United States.
